Yi Xu

I am a 3rd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Shuigeng Zhou. I received my my bachelor's degree from Shanghai University.

I work in the areas of video understanding, and in particular, I am interested in combining spatial and temporal information for learning spatiotemporal structure in videos. I have worked as a research intern in Bilibili AI group and summer intern in Microsoft Bing Ads group. I received the 2019 Intel Fellowship.
